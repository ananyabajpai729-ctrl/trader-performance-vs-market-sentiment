# Key Insights

## 1. Profitability differs across sentiment
- Traders earn higher average profits during Greed periods
- Win rate is slightly higher during Fear periods

## 2. Risk-taking increases during Fear
- Trade sizes are highest during Fear conditions
- Larger trades do not necessarily lead to higher profits

## 3. Neutral markets offer weaker opportunities
- Lowest PnL observed during Neutral sentiment
- Indicates lack of strong directional movement

## 4. High-size traders dominate profits
- Majority of profits come from high trade size segment
- Low-size traders show minimal returns despite reasonable win rates

## 5. Market conditions impact segments differently
- High-size traders benefit significantly during Greed periods
- Low-size traders show limited gains across all conditions
