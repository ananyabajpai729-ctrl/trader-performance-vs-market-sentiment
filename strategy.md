# Strategy Recommendations

## 1. Adjust exposure based on sentiment
- Increase exposure during Greed periods to capture larger price movements
- Reduce activity during Neutral periods due to lower opportunities

## 2. Manage risk during Fear periods
- Avoid excessive trade sizes during Fear conditions
- Focus on consistency rather than aggressive positions

## 3. Segment-based approach
- High-capital traders can leverage Greed markets for higher returns
- Low-capital traders should be more selective and avoid overtrading

## 4. Focus on efficiency, not just win rate
- High win rate does not guarantee high profitability
- Trade size and timing play a critical role
